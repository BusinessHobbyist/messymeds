About us

Some people ask "Why?"

We're all about asking "why not?"! After working in the medical field for several years, we started asking ourselves why nobody had branded themselves with the hilarious slang that we all know and love. I mean, some of the stuff we say is absolutely hilarious! From the 'boyfriend' on the floor to the boss going 'A Wall' - there's no shortage of material.

After a while, we just couldn't take it anymore; so we decided to make some logos, throw up a website and see how it goes.

If you like what we have to offer, tell somebody :)
